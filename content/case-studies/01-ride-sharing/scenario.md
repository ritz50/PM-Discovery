# Case: Should we enter the ride-sharing market?

## Context

You're a PM at a mobility startup. Leadership asks you to assess entering ride-sharing in a mid-size city where two incumbents operate.

## Data

- City population: 2M; daily commuters: 400K
- Incumbent A: 60% share, known for reliability
- Incumbent B: 30% share, cheaper
- User interviews (n=12): top pains are wait time (8/12), surge pricing (6/12), driver quality (4/12)
- Your company's strength: fleet management tech for B2B logistics

## Your task

1. Frame the problem/opportunity
2. Outline a discovery plan for 2 weeks
3. Recommend pursue, pivot, or kill with rationale
