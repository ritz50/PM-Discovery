# Case: AI Meeting Summarization

## Context

Your B2B SaaS product has meeting recordings. Sales wants an AI summary feature for enterprise accounts.

## Data

- 200 enterprise users record meetings weekly
- Support tickets: "hard to find action items" (40% of recording-related tickets)
- Prototype demo gets positive reactions but no commitment data
- Engineering estimates: 6 weeks for v1
- Legal flagged: PII in transcripts

## Your task

1. Frame whether AI is the right solution
2. Define eval criteria for "good enough" summary
3. Design a 2-week experiment before full build
