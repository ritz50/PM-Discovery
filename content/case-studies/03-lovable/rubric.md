# Rubric — Lovable Case Study

## Strong answer includes

- References **Mom Test** / past behavior — not opinion questions
- Names functional + emotional jobs (e.g. ship MVP fast, feel capable as non-coder)
- Artifacts: persona, empathy map, journey map, synthesis themes
- Competitive questions that explore switching and workarounds

## Model answer outline

Good questions ask what users **did** last time they built an app, what tools they used, and what almost stopped them — mirroring switch interview logic. JTBD: "When I have an idea, I want to validate it quickly without hiring engineers, so I can test market fit." After interviews: affinity map → 2-3 opportunity areas → persona for "indie builder" vs "PM prototyper." Additional questions: "Tell me about the last time you abandoned a side project — what happened?"
