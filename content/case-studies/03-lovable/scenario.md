# Case: Lovable.dev discovery (helloPM Session 2)

*From helloPM Product Discovery & Research Session 2 — Lovable product case study*

## Context

Lovable is an AI-powered app builder that grew rapidly post-2022. helloPM walks through how the team did discovery to understand builders and no-code users.

## Discovery approach (from session)

Interview guide themes — focus on **past behavior**, not hypotheticals:

1. How do you currently build a new app or feature?
2. If using Lovable — how did you use it in your last project? If not — what tools did you use?
3. What was hardest about that process?
4. Wrap-up: Any feedback on Lovable or AI app builders? Question you expected us to ask that we didn't?

## Your task

1. Why are these good discovery questions (vs "Would you use X?")?
2. What **jobs to be done** might Lovable users have (functional, emotional, social)?
3. Draft 3 additional interview questions for a competitor to Lovable.
4. What artifacts would you create after 8 interviews (persona, journey map, etc.)?
