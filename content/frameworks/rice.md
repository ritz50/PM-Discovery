# RICE Prioritization

Score initiatives to decide what to build next.

## Formula

**RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort**

## Components

| Factor | Question | Scale |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Reach** | How many users affected per quarter? | Number |
| **Impact** | How much does it move the goal? | 0.25 (minimal) to 3 (massive) |
| **Confidence** | How sure are we? | 50%–100% |
| **Effort** | Person-months to ship | Number |

## Steps

1. List candidate initiatives
2. Score each factor with evidence
3. Calculate RICE score
4. Rank and discuss — score is input, not verdict
5. Communicate tradeoffs to stakeholders

## Tips

- Use consistent time horizons for Reach
- Confidence drops without discovery evidence
- Effort includes design, eng, and PM time
- Re-score as you learn

## When NOT to use RICE

- Strategic bets with unmeasurable reach
- Compliance or security must-dos
- Dependencies that block everything else
