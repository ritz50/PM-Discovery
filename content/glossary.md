# Product Discovery Glossary

## A

**Assumption mapping** — Visualizing beliefs about users, problems, and solutions; ranking by risk and uncertainty to prioritize validation.

## C

**Continuous discovery** — Weekly touchpoints with customers to iteratively test assumptions (Teresa Torres).

**Concept testing** — Showing a solution concept to users before building to gauge desirability.

## D

**Discovery** — Learning what to build before committing engineering resources.

**Double Diamond** — Diverge/converge model for problem then solution exploration.

## H

**Hypothesis-driven development** — Stating testable beliefs and designing experiments to validate or invalidate them.

## J

**Jobs to Be Done (JTBD)** — Framework focusing on the progress customers seek, not demographics.

## M

**MVP** — Minimum viable product: smallest thing that tests a key hypothesis.

## O

**Opportunity Solution Tree (OST)** — Maps desired outcomes → opportunities → solutions → experiments.

## P

**Problem-solution fit** — Evidence that a real problem exists and your solution addresses it.

**PRD** — Product Requirements Document; translates discovery into buildable specs.

## R

**RICE** — Prioritization score: Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort.

## S

**Switch interview** — JTBD interview exploring a past purchase/adoption decision.

**SM-2** — Spaced repetition algorithm for optimizing review intervals.

## T

**TAM/SAM/SOM** — Total/Serviceable/Obtainable market sizing framework.
