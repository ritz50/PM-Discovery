# Personas & Segments

Personas humanize research findings. They are **research-based archetypes**, not fictional characters.

## Proto-persona vs research persona

- **Proto-persona** — Team hypothesis before research (quick alignment)
- **Research persona** — Built from interview patterns (evidence-based)

## What to include

- Goals and motivations (JTBD-aligned)
- Pain points and workarounds
- Context of use
- Behavioral patterns (not just demographics)

## Anti-personas

Users you explicitly do **not** build for. Prevents scope creep.

## Key takeaway

Update personas as you learn. A stale persona is worse than none.
