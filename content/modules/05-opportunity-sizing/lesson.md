# Opportunity Sizing

Estimate if an opportunity is large enough to justify investment.

## TAM / SAM / SOM

- **TAM** — Total addressable market (everyone who could use it)
- **SAM** — Serviceable addressable market (your segment and geography)
- **SOM** — Serviceable obtainable market (realistic capture in 1-3 years)

## Bottom-up vs top-down

- **Top-down** — Start with industry reports, narrow down (fast, less credible)
- **Bottom-up** — # customers × price × frequency (slower, more defensible)

## Opportunity assessment

Combine sizing with strategic fit, competitive dynamics, and discovery evidence.

## Key takeaway

Precision matters less than order of magnitude. Is this a $1M or $100M opportunity?
