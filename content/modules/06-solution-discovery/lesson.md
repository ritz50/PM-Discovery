# Solution Discovery

Once problems are validated, explore **how** to solve them before full build.

## Ideation principles

- Diverge before converging
- Build on opportunities from research, not opinions
- Quantity before quality in brainstorms

## Prototype fidelity

| Fidelity | When | Examples |
|----------|------|----------|
| Low | Early concept test | Paper sketches, Figma wireframes |
| Medium | Flow validation | Clickable prototype |
| High | Pre-launch test | Working prototype, wizard of oz |

## Concept testing

Show the concept → observe reactions → measure intent → iterate.

## Key takeaway

The goal is learning speed, not polish. Match fidelity to your riskiest assumption.
